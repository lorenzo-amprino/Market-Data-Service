# Use seeded and on-demand Universe growth

Market Data Service v1 will not import a global catalog of all investable instruments by default. Instead, the Universe will start from a small configured seed set and grow on demand: explicit Discovery Search may synchronously query Data Providers for candidate instruments or listings, while admin, portfolio-driven, or user-initiated Instrument Discovery Requests trigger acquisition of selected candidates and may add them to the Universe with bounded ingestion and backfill when requested. This keeps a personal-use deployment lightweight and avoids provider load, rate-limit pressure, storage growth, and maintenance work for instruments that no consumer actually needs, while still supporting interactive onboarding of missing instruments.
